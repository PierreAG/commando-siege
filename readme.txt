Warcraft III siege map for 4 players. The goal is for all 4 players to reunite to kill the last boss

Red [Thrall] - Wc3 style player with workers and units. 

Blue [Blademaster] - Melee support responsable for helping characters reach eachother

Teal [Shadow hunter] - Healer support with croud control

Purple [Tauren Chieftain] - Heavy hitting character
